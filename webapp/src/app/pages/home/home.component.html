Welcom to Fabian's Blog!

This is Fabian's Blog. A blog, where I, Fabian, will elaborate and discuss about topics like webdevelopment.

I have quiet some experience in the MEAN stack. However in my opinion the "E" should probably removed, since Express can in today's world definitely be abstracted by some higher level framework for the easy of use.

What can I expect from this blog?

The coolest thing is that this blog is open source. Anything developed on for the website you are currently visiting can be reused by you for your own needs. You will be able to interact with me and ask me questions like:

  • How should Thing X be implemented?
  • Why did you implement Thing X like the way you did?
  • Why don't you add Thing X to your website, so that I will be able to reuse the solution for my personal project?
